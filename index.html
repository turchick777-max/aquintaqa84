How it Works

Intake & Stabilization
Coarse protection and flow stabilization; baseline conditioning as needed. No heavy pre-treatment trains.
Gas-Sorbent Dosing
Carrier gas with proprietary sorbent is introduced into the flow to form active capture centers.
Sorption (Contactor)
Mass transfer: salts, hardness and organics bind to the sorbent surface under mild conditions.
Separation (Phase split)
Purified water is separated from loaded sorbent by mechanical/physical means; no RO or distillation.
Mineralization & Polishing
Final tuning to drinking profile; disinfection on demand; output is ready-to-drink water.
